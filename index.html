The signal problem

Traditional B2B networking tells you what a company does — not how it operates.

01 · LINKEDIN

A feed, not a filter.

Sales Navigator surfaces job titles, headcount, and funding round. None of that tells you whether a prospective partner shares your decision tempo, risk posture, or governance style — the things that determine whether a partnership survives the second year.

02 · TRADE EVENTS

€2,000 for a stack of cards.

Three days, a booth, a lanyard. The signal you actually need — how does this company operate behind the booth banner? — isn't there. You leave with contacts and no compatibility data.

Frequently asked

Direct answers.

If something here doesn't cover your question, write to support@minerva.it. Italian and English.

Is Minerva only for Italian companies?

Yes. V1 is Italian-only. Companies must be registered with an Italian Camera di Commercio. International expansion is on the Phase 2 roadmap, starting with cross-border matching for Italian PMI exporting to the EU.

How is “cultural compatibility” different from sector matching?

Sector matching tells you what a company does. Cultural compatibility tells you how they do it — decision tempo, risk posture, governance discipline, communication register. In our experience, the how predicts whether a partnership survives the second year better than the what.

What is verified before a profile goes live?

P.IVA registration, codice ATECO, registered office, and confirmation that the contact person is an authorized representative of the company. Verification typically completes within twenty-four hours.

Why €39 per month?

Premium pricing is defensible against a single trade event (€2,000+) or a Sales Navigator seat (€99+ per month), while remaining accessible to the smaller PMI we built this for. Free tier covers the core matchmaking; Premium adds depth and exports.

Is matching reciprocal?

Yes. Both companies must indicate interest before contact details are exchanged. There is no cold outreach inside Minerva — by design. It keeps signal-to-noise high for both sides.

Can I delete my company profile?

Yes — from the account settings, in two clicks. GDPR erasure requests are honoured within thirty days. Match history and exchanged messages can be exported before deletion.
